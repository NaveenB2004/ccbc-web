Recent Functions of the School Development Society

Establish and implement safety committees for the protection of school children.
Installing CCTV cameras to protect the discipline of the school children and the safety of the school.
Construction of safety wall in school garden.
Construction of nets for internal security.
Providing necessary facilities for the development and education of the school and providing necessary support to the principal.
Awareness programs about non-school children.
Developing and protecting the schoolâ€™s physical resources, conducting educational seminars, enhancing library facilities, educating and educating both parents and students about the lifestyle of the area in accordance with the policies of the Ministry of Education.
Improving the musical, dance sections and focusing them on the children.
Obtaining the support of the Student Union such as conducting school shramadana, renovation of buildings and classrooms, finding and cleaning sanitary facilities and providing financial assistance to the children of low income, foreign education, sports competitions etc.
Our aim is to build up a better society with the support of all the community, by providing education, social, cultural, health and safety, sports and higher education to the children of the school.
